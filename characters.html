Hulk

Bruce Banner

Exposed to heavy doses of gamma radiation, scientist Bruce Banner transforms into the mean, green rage machine called the Hulk.

Thanos


The Mad Titan Thanos quests across the universe in search of the Infinity Stones. Using the power of the Infinity Stones, Thanos believes he can ultimately save the universe by wiping out half of its population.

Iron Man

Tony Stark

Genius. Billionaire. Philanthropist. Tony Stark's confidence is only matched by his high-flying abilities as the hero called Iron Man.

Hawkeye

Clint Barton

An expert marksman and fighter, works for S.H.I.E.L.D. as a special agent. The archer known as Hawkeye also boasts a strong moral compass that at times leads him astray from his direct orders.

Captain America

Steve Rogers

Recipient of the Super-Soldier serum, World War II hero Steve Rogers fights for American ideals as one of the world’s mightiest heroes and continues his fight in the present as an Avenger and untiring sentinel of liberty.

Thor

Thor Odinson

The son of Odin uses his mighty abilities as the God of Thunder to protect his home Asgard and planet Earth alike. He wields the power of the ancient Asgardians to fight evil throughout the Nine Realms and beyond.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff

One of S.H.I.E.L.D.’s most deadly assassins and a frequent member of the Avengers. She strives to make up for her bad past by helping the world, even if that means getting her hands dirty in the process.

Scarlet Witch

Wanda Maximoff

Wanda Maximoff aimed her anger over her parents death at Tony Stark, which eventually led her to gain powers at HYDRA, which drove her to Ultron-and switched sides to do what is right to help the world..

Doctor Strange

Stephen Strange

Formerly a renowned surgeon, now serves as the Sorcerer Supreme-Earth’s newest Master of Mystic arts by protecting against magical and mystical threats, possessing vast magical knowledge and skills to call upon.

Ant Man

Scott Lang

When ex-criminal Scott Lang turned to his illicit skills in order to save his daughter’s life, he unexpectedly became the size-shifting, insect-commanding Super Hero known as Ant-Man!

Captain Marvel

Carol Danvers

Near death, Carol Danvers was transformed into a powerful warrior for the Kree. Now, returning to Earth years later, she must remember her past in order to to prevent a full invasion by shapeshifting aliens, the Skrulls.

Black Panther

T'Challa

T'Challa is the king of the secretive and highly advanced African nation of Wakanda - as well as the powerful warrior known as the Black Panther. He protects his people as the latest in a legacy line of Black Panther warriors.

Nick Fury

Nicholas Joseph Fury

In the years following the Cold War, Nick Fury wrestles with his sense of purpose within S.H.I.E.L.D. When Nick crosses paths with Captain Marvel, they become Earth's only hope of stopping a Skrull invasion.

Star Lord

Peter Quill

Would-be thief Peter Quill journeys through space as Star-Lord, the leader of the Guardians of the Galaxy, brings a sassy sense of humor while protecting the universe from any and all threats.

Vision

Vision

The android called Vision defies physics and fights as an Avenger with the power of density manipulation and his flawless computer brain. He does his best to understand and control the Mind Stone in his forehead as an Avenger.
